Built for real-life transitions

HerFinance AI helps women navigate income shifts, childcare costs, debt pressure, and savings goals — with clarity, confidence, and a personalized roadmap.

💜

Why This Matters

Life transitions can create sudden financial instability — especially for single moms and divorced women. Most budgeting tools assume stable income and shared household support, which doesn’t match reality.

  • Income changes + unpredictable expenses
  • Childcare and essentials take priority
  • Debt builds fast and feels overwhelming
  • Generic advice doesn’t work for real life
✨

Our Solution

A supportive AI assistant that turns your financial snapshot into a step-by-step recovery plan — built around your situation, dependents, and goals.

  • Personalized monthly budget you can actually follow
  • Debt payoff strategy with a clear debt-free timeline
  • Emergency fund roadmap for stability
  • Scenario planning (“what if income drops?”)
